Why Us

We do the hard work

"Love You More" is Portland's premier matchmaking service dedicated to facilitating genuine connections and helping individuals find their perfect match. With a deep understanding of the complexities of modern dating, we specialize in bringing together like-minded individuals who are seeking meaningful and lasting relationships. Our team of experienced matchmakers takes a personalized approach to each client, getting to know their unique preferences, values, and aspirations. Through meticulous screening and thoughtful analysis, we carefully pair compatible singles, ensuring that every match has the potential to blossom into something truly special.

At "Love You More," we believe that everyone deserves to find their soulmate, and we are committed to guiding our clients on their journey to love and companionship. Whether you're a busy professional struggling to find time for dating, a recent transplant to the city looking to expand your social circle, or someone who simply hasn't had luck with traditional dating methods, we're here to offer support, guidance, and expertise every step of the way. Our mission is to make the search for love an enjoyable and fulfilling experience, empowering individuals to embrace vulnerability, open their hearts, and ultimately find the person they've been searching for.
